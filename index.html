TiE Grad Premier League is an initiative aiming to establish a pool of real-time solution hackers to build a sustainable knowledge ecosystem in Hyderabad in addition to providing a platform for engaging these solution hackers with corporates, domain experts, local industries and academic institutions.


Get opportunities to work with the best in the industry on cutting edge technology

TIE GPL 2019


Stage 1 - Orientation & Filtering

a. The first stage will involve a competition in the partnered colleges and will act as the first stage in the filtering process. On a designated day, an orientation session will be conducted in every college and a preliminary assessment test will be conducted with an emphasis on Aptitude, Quantitative analysis, Basic C language and Java.

b. The top 100 students from this stage will proceed to the second stage of the GPL.



Stage II - Code Assessment & Behaviour Evaluation

a. The stage 2 contestants will be given a login code to take the programming test on online platform..

b. Top students from each college will be sent to the next round.

c. An online platform will be used to conduct a behaviour evaluation for the selected students a detailed report of each student would be created to understand the behaviour traits in various areas.


Stage III - Bidding Process

The students who have qualified the third stage will be eligible for bidding by TiE Charter Member Mentors. The highest bidders get to own teams of these students who will take part in the Grand Challenge, which is the final round.
