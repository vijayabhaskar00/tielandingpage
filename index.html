GPL is a Marathon Hackathon, for students who are academically brilliant or have deep expertise in specific areas. This Hackathon will help them showcase their prowess, while the students oriented entrepreneurially will focus on B- plan competitions. As a part of Hackathon, each institutional member can nominate a maximum of 5 students in various areas of expertise who will randomly be picked by the team owners through a bidding process.
These selected students will be further groomed by a coach (subject matter expert in these areas. Post which, a minimum of 10 teams will compete with each other on real-world business problems curated by large corporate, viz. EY, PWC, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft, Qualcomm, Google etc.
