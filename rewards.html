About GPL

Information Technology is one avenue that is required in every sector and over the past couple of decades, has been increasingly used to solve various problems all over the world. In an attempt to establish a pool of rigorously curated; real-time solution hackers to real-world industrial challenges, TiE Hyderabad has initiated a program called Graduates’ Premier League (GPL). This program aims to provide a platform for engaging these solution hackers with Corporations, Domain Experts, Local Industries and Academic Institutions of the region, apart from building a sustainable, resilient and dynamic knowledge ecosystem in Hyderabad through this pool of solution hackers.


GPL aims to select five students from 25 colleges and have these 125 students will attempt to solve real-world problems during a 36-hour hackathon where the participants will come up with a solution architecture for those problems. Out of these, the top 50 will be eligible for various opportunities from TiE Hyderabad in addition to having a mentor and more.


The Graduates’ Premier League is open to final year CSE/IT engineering students, as it deals exclusively with the IT sector. If you are a fourth-year CSE/IT student, you can sign up *here*. After a first round of elimination, the top 100 students from each college will then proceed to further rounds, before the top 5 are selected from every college.


If you are a tech-geek who loves to code to solve problems, this is how you can start solving them!
